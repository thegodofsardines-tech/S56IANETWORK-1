CREATION (LORE)

Shortly before the Events of Site-19, a group of Internal Security Members in the Intelligence Branch had discovered intel of Chaos Insurgency Networks and communications that traced to a raid or attack on Research Site-19. However, due to the Intelligence Branch lacking overall respect from the Department and appropriate fundings, the case was voided by Internal Defense Authorities. The Director of the case, Destiny Erorath, gathered the investigators of the report and swiftly retired from the Internal Security Department as a whole, and made an unofficial group called the "Intelligence Agency". Shortly after this, Site-19 suffered extreme containment breaches including Keter class & Euclid, which Internal Defenses later traced to a Chaos Insurgency Raid which was detected by a group of investigators within the Internal Security Department earlier that month, whom later retired due to lack of respect and funding. Following this, Site Command made the Intelligence Agency official within Site-19 and sent a formal request to the O5 Council via representatives of the Department and its official approval over all of the Foundation's installations. After a short confidential interview & background check on the Intelligence Agency's Director,Destiny Erorath, which was conducted personally by the O5 Council themselves in order to ensure competence for the role, the Department/Agency was approved under O5 oversight. O5-3 being the Project Director and Overseer.

FOLLOW UP

Following the approval of the Intelligence Agency the Department’s authority was immediately emplaced within the Foundations Installations & Network, the Department being disguised as a variety of public Departments during field operations to personnel under Clearance Level 4. Additionally, rumors of the Department inevitably began to arise to personnel under the required Clearance Level to have direct access and knowledge of the Intelligence Agency. However, few of these rumors became genuine, reliable theories within the eternity of Foundation personnel Clearance Level 3 and below, the select few beginning to cross the line between “Rumor” and “Evidence” were swiftly erased. Furthermore, during an OVERWATCH Council rescue mission of which the Intelligence Agency was utilized for Intelligence & Counter Intelligence operations, Destiny Erorath the Director of the Intelligence Agency was deployed to Surveillance Site-04 located █████████ ████ ██ ███ ██████████ ██ ██████, and it was planned for him to be relocated shortly afterwards to █████████ ████-██, otherwise known as “█████████ ██”. Shortly after Destiny arrived at Site-04, an organized Chaos Insurgency Network attack was launched on the area, rendering any forms of communication unreachable. It is unknown exactly what happened during this time, but Destiny insists a bystander Internal Defenses Representative by the name of Kiara Valentine swiftly saved Destiny from a Insurgent ambush by swiftly relocating the I.A Director to an emergency entrance port into Site-04. After a thorough background check on Kiara Valentine and a long thorough interview with both the Director of the Intelligence Agency & Project Director O5-3, Kiara Valentine was shortly after promoted to Deputy Director of Intelligence.
